The new, simple way to predict live data
Have a live source of data?
Do you need to:
  • Monitor it for outliers and extended anomalies,
  • Remove distracting noise, or
  • Predict it a few minutes forward in time?
You can achieve this by:
  1. Publishing a scalar value, and
  2. Publishing a scalar value again, and
  3. ... repeating over an over again to generate a stream.
See the instructions.
Do this for long enough and competing time series algorithms will conspire to generate a distribution of near-future values on multiple horizons, and other useful diagnostics such as implied z-scores that tell you how surprising each data point was relative to the community predictions. You can even request a crowd estimate of a joint distribution in two or three dimensions.
Have a good algorithm?
Think you can predict covid news or the distribution of stock price movements? See predicting and crawling instructions. Streams with significant monetary rewards are coming soon so fire up your algorithms now.

Contact Us

There are no silly questions, only improvable documentation!

Quora

  1. At quora click Add Question button - red and top right.
  2. Add subject "microprediction" in addition to others that may be relevant
  3. Try to make your issue or comment or question as clear and, ideally, reproducible, as you can.
  4. Consider whether it should also be asked as an issue at microprediction repo on github.

Issues

It is more than fine to post an issue pointing out that documentation is unclear, or could be improved.
  1. Upgrade! pip install --upgrade microprediction, and see if the problem is fixed already.
  2. Add issues at microprediction.
  3. As above, reproducibility is highly desireable.
  4. Consider creating a small colab notebook to illustrate your question and at the same time provide a fast way for someone to respond.
  5. Consider creating a gist to illustrate your question, if applicable, for the same reasons.

Teacher mode

If using PythonAnywhere to run your crawler you have another option.
  1. From your home page, locate the "Teacher" tab
  2. Add teacher name "microprediction"
  3. THIS WILL GRANT US ACCESS TO YOUR CODE, LOGS etc
  4. Also follow one of the steps above and reference your account on PA.

General career questions

  1. Send CVs to careers@microprediction.org
  2. Be aware that your CV may be forwarded to companies sponsoring contests, such as Intech Investments.
  3. Some employers may appreciate evidence that you are willing and capable of performing tasks that relate to real world work. Suggested reading: Dorothy, You're not in Kaggle Anymore.
